.     - Any Character except new line
\d    - Digit(0-9)
\D    - Not a digit
\w    - Word Character (a-z, A-Z, 0-9, _)
\W    - Not a word character
\s    - Whitespace(space, tab, new line)
\S    - Not Whitespace

\b    - Word Boundary
\B    - Not a word Boundary
^     - Beginning of the string
$     - End of the string

[]    - Matching characters or rules in the list
[^ ]  - Not Matching characters ir rules in the list
|     - OR
()    - Group

Quantifiers:
*     - 0 or More
+     - 1 or More
?     - 0 or One
{3}   - Exact amount of occurrences
{3,4} - From {x to y} of occurrences
